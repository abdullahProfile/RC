About Our Firm

Rosenstein Chicatelli PLLC is a leading real estate law firm in the Greater Capital Region, providing trusted legal counsel through experience, dedication, and client-focused service. Our attorneys are highly skilled in residential and commercial real estate transactions, elder law, estate planning, and small business advisement, supported by a responsive team committed to exceptional client care. Through our affiliate, Marathon Title Agency, Inc., an agent for Chicago Title Insurance Company, we also offer reliable title insurance services to help protect our clients’ real estate investments.
